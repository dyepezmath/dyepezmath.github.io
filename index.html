I am an IDA Postdoctoral Fellow at Princeton University mentored by Nick Katz. I received my PhD in 2024 from Wayne State University under the supervision of Luca Candelori and Vladimir Chernyak.

My work primarily concerns algebraic geometry, in particular, I am interested in studying the decomposition of the underlying vector bundles in logarithmic connections that arise from monodromy representations. However, I am also happy to work in any other area of mathematics that friends and colleagues find interesting.
